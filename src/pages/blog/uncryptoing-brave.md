So lets begin from the start

![](/assets/uncrypto-brave-guide/start.png)

Open Customize

![](/assets/uncrypto-brave-guide/customize-menu.png)

scroll down

![](/assets/uncrypto-brave-guide/scrolled-down.png)

Click on top sites and disable that too
![](/assets/uncrypto-brave-guide/top-sites-disabled.png)

Go to cards and turn those off too
![](/assets/uncrypto-brave-guide/cards-off.png)

Exit and scroll down
![](/assets/uncrypto-brave-guide/exit-scroll.png)

Turn this off too
![](/assets/uncrypto-brave-guide/turn-off-extra.png)

Open the settings `brave://settings` or the hamburger
and turn of brave rewards
![](/assets/uncrypto-brave-guide/rewards-off.png)

And thats it! now basically all crypto related things are gone and you can focus on browsing brave made it quite easy to do this actually.
